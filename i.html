Biography

Kobe Bryant is a American basketball shooting guard who plays used to play for Los Angeles Lakers and the Ameircan national team. He was born at August 23, 1978, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States. Kobe was known for his incredible speed, dribbling ability, and goal-scoring prowess and of course his mamba mentality.
